Richard Monyei, better known by my stage name Rik Hamale , is a rapper born on December 26, 1994, in Lagos, Nigeria. I am also one of the founders of Rik Hamale Music, a record label and music production company that handles my music. I took interest in rapping while in boarding school because I had access to lyric books which were a trend then.
I began rapping when he was 16 and started posting some of my materials online. I released my debut single, titled More Money, in 2016. The song, containing lyrics about a hustlers mindset has gathered a lot of streams streams on YouTube and Spotify. I became more popular after being one of the grand finalists at the 2020 Hennessy VS Class Competition. My second EP, Slick Rik 2, was released in 2021 and has thousands of plays on Streaming platforms since the first month of its release.
I also have a social media program which I call Slick Mix Series, in this program I rap over beats of popular mainstream music and this program has garnered thousands of fans for Me on my social media. More info about these can be found on my instagram page @rik_hamale
